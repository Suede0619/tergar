## 5 Key Topics for Discussion

### 1. Platform Recommendation: WordPress vs Squarespace

**RFP mentions:** "Recommend platform & architecture (WordPress, Squarespace (have template), or other) with an eye toward: ease of upkeep, future flexibility, integration with booking system (Retreat Guru), integration with donation systems"

**My Input:**
- **Recommend WordPress with ENFOLD theme** over Squarespace
- **Why:** Nonprofit donation tools (GiveWP), Retreat Guru integration flexibility, superior SEO (critical for limited marketing budget), platform ownership (no vendor lock-in), future scalability (member portal, online courses, multilingual)
- **Squarespace limitations:** Requires Commerce plan ($52/mo vs $33/mo) for full donations, limited booking integration, slower SEO, vendor lock-in, constraints future growth
- **Cost comparison:** Similar 5-year cost (~$2,835 WordPress vs $3,120 Squarespace), but WordPress delivers more features
- **Ease-of-use concern addressed:** ENFOLD drag-and-drop builder + 1-2 hour training (included in scope) = manageable for non-technical staff

**Question to ask:** What's Tergar's current platform, and what are the biggest pain points with the existing site?

**RFP asks for:** "High-trust donation page design, seamless integration with donation/payment tools, Gift Pyramid graphics, Donor cultivation one-pager"

**My Input:**
- **Capital campaigns need robust donor management:** Recurring donations (monthly sustainers), donor database (track major donors), campaign progress tracking (gift pyramid thermometer), offline donation tracking (checks/wire transfers), tax-deductible receipts
- **WordPress solution:** GiveWP plugin ($0-199/year) = purpose-built for nonprofits, includes all features above

**Question to ask:** Does Tergar have existing donor database/CRM? How are offline donations (checks, wire transfers) currently tracked? What donation platforms are they currently using or considering?
